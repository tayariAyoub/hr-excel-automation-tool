# Customization Services

This repository demonstrates the standard version of the HR + Excel Automation
Tool. Small companies can hire me to adapt it to their workflow.

## Available customizations

- Company name, logo, colors, and branded Excel reports
- Additional employee fields and departments
- Overtime, bonuses, deductions, and custom salary rules
- Weekly, monthly, or yearly management reports
- Charts and HR dashboards
- Different currencies and languages
- Employee search, editing, and deletion
- Importing existing employee data from Excel or CSV
- Windows desktop interface instead of a command-line menu
- Installation help, documentation, and training

## Typical delivery

1. Discuss the company's current Excel workflow.
2. Confirm the required fields and calculations.
3. Build and test the customized tool.
4. Deliver the Python application, Excel templates, and instructions.
